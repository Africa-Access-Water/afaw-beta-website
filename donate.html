Join Us in Making a Difference

Two sites in Mphande and Rufunsa communities have been earmark to install Solar powered water for productive use- all year irrigation, drinking and animals. The two site will help at least 2,300 people with clean drinkning water, at 100 smale-scale farmers/ households (1000 people) growing different crops with increased income and food secure, 1,000 animals. We are looking for US$93,000 for the two sites.

Your contribution directly supports the construction of solar-powered water systems for rural Zambian communities. Together, we can ensure clean drinking water, food security, and lasting development.

All donations are securely processed via Stripe & DPO Group. Thank you for empowering change.
